✦ Lore ✦

"A little kingdom of mischief, mended each day with small kindnesses."

The Mischief Meter

Every kindness you do for yourself, every quiet rebellion of joy — Loki notices, and counts. Mischief points are not currency; they are proof. Proof that today, you stayed.

His mood shifts with you: playful, smug, proud, sulking. Tend to him by tending to yourself.

Mood & Streak

The mood pill at the top of the home page reads Loki's heart in the moment. The streak counts the consecutive days you've returned — break it gently, and he forgives. Break it carelessly, and he sulks.

The Realms

  • Thor's Water — the hydration counter. Eight cups a day, and the storm-god grins.
  • Odin's Apothecary — the medicine tracker. Tick each one off and the All-Father nods.
  • Loki's Bond — daily endeavours: study, art, language. Three small fields, watered every day.
  • Loki's Library — the books you've read, the books you mean to.
  • Frigga's Spellbook — small spells and rituals, the queen's quiet wisdom.
  • Sif's Cookbook — recipes worth keeping, hearths worth lighting.
  • Freya's Treasury — gold counted, budgets weighed, wishes saved toward.
  • Heimdall's Observatory — he watches your care history, never judges, only sees.
  • Sigyn's Dungeon — confess your transgressions to the patient one. Each costs ten ✦. Serve the punishment, reclaim them.

Trophy Hall

Every achievement you earn across the realms is collected in the Trophy Hall. Locked ones tell you how to unlock them — never to scold, only to invite.

Your Data

Everything is stored in your browser. Nothing leaves. To keep it safe, use the Backup all realms button on the home page (or the per-realm backup on each page) to download a JSON file you can restore later.
